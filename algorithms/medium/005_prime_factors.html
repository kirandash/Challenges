Find Prime Factors of a number

Create a function which takes a number as input and returns all of it's prime factors.
Read more about prime numbers and Prime factors at https://www.mathsisfun.com/prime-factorization.html

Hint: A Prime Number is: a whole number greater than 1 that can not be made by multiplying other whole number.
"Prime Factorization" is finding which prime numbers multiply together to make the original number.

Ex: 
                findPrimeFactors(12) ➞ [2, 2, 3]
                findPrimeFactors(147) ➞ [3, 7, 7]
                findPrimeFactors(17) ➞ [17]
